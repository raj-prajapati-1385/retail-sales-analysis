- Python: The primary programming language.
- pandas: Used for data manipulation and analysis.
- numpy: Used for numerical operations (often used in conjunction with pandas).
- matplotlib: Used for creating visualizations.
- seaborn: Used for creating aesthetically pleasing statistical graphics (built on top of matplotlib).
- Google Colab: The environment where the notebook is being run.
- google.colab.files: Used for uploading files to the Colab environment.
- IPython.display: Used for displaying images and other rich output in the notebook.
